About Me

I am a designer and researcher currently living in Chicago, Illinois. I am a graduate student in Human-Computer Interaction at DePaul University and a student at Northwestern University's Coding Boot Camp. My research interests include human-computer interaction and health technology.


During my free time, I enjoy binge-watching documentaries, volunteering, traveling, obstacle course races, and exploring the wonders of nature. Back in California, I have 5 dogs (Poodle terrier, Chihuahua mix, Shih Tzu and 2 Pekingeses), turtle, bunny, and a partridge in a pear-tree.
